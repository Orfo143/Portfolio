About me

My name is Victor. Some time ago I needed a website, so I needed to come up with a layout and I turned to ’specialists’, but no one could draw the layout that I would like ... Then I made a decision and learned web design. Yes, at first it was interesting, but after working on this business for about a year, I realized that I was not interested in it and I did not recognize some of my layouts that were passed on to the layout.

I create both simple landing pages and complex sites such as stores, corporate sites, portals, but I only take on projects that are interesting to me. That is, if the proposed project is not interesting for me to make up, then it is unlikely that I will undertake it. How interesting layouts can you offer me?
